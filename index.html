The Sage of Rakab Ganj

Principal Gyani Dyal Singh

Gyani Dyal Singh was one of the leading figures in the world of Gurmat Sangeet. He served as the Principal of the Gurmat Vidyalay at Rakab Ganj in Delhi since 1967.
